We have chosen to get married in Wales in the heart of the Brecon Beacons. As you may know, Wales is a special location for both of us. We are getting married at Tall John's Manor House.



If travelling by plane, the nearest airports are:

Cardiff: 52 miles (1.5 hour drive)
Bristol: 67 miles (1.5 hour drive)
Birmingham: 107 miles (2 hour drive)
London Heathrow: 155 miles (3 hour drive)

Trains, car rental and taxis are available at all airports. We recommend pre-booking transport.



If travelling by car, please enter the following address for Tall John's House into your satnav:

Tall John's House, Llangasty Tal-y-llyn, Brecon, LD3 7PX

This will bring you to the rough area but not to the exact location. When you get to Bwlch (a small village between Brecon and Crickhowell) please follow these instructions.



If travelling by train, the nearest stations are:

Abergavenny: 14 miles (27 min drive)
Newport: 32 miles (50 min drive)
Cardiff: 49 miles (1.25 hour drive)

Taxis are available at all train stations. We recommend pre-booking transport and can provide details of a local taxi service if required.
