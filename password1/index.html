Our Venue



We have chosen to get married in Wales in the heart of the Brecon Beacons. This National Park was established in 1957 and covers 519 square miles in total. As you may know, Wales is a special location for both of us and we couldn't imagine getting married in any other location. In our opinion, it's the perfect place to get married.

We are getting married at Tall John's Manor House. This venue looks across to the Black Mountains on one side and over to the Brecon Beacons on the other. Tall John's Manor House was built in 1588 and underwent substantial renovations in the 19th and 20th centuries by members of the British Navy.
